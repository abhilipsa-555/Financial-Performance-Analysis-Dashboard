## 📌 Overview

An interactive *financial analytics dashboard* built in Power BI to track and analyze *sales performance, revenue contribution, and profitability* across multiple dimensions like location, product, and business segments.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

✨ *Time Intelligence Analysis*

* Year-to-Date (YTD)
* Quarter-to-Date (QTD)
* Month-to-Date (MTD)

📊 *Business KPIs*

* Gross Profit
* Net Sales
* Profit Margin %
* Revenue Contribution %

🎯 *Interactive Filtering*

* Year, Quarter, Month
* Segment & Business Categories

🌍 *Detailed Analysis*

* Country → City level insights
* Category → Product → Sub-product drilldowns

---

## 🧮 Key Calculations

DAX
Gross Sales = Units Sold × Sale Price
Net Sales = Gross Sales − Discounts
Gross Profit = Net Sales − COGS
Profit Margin % = Gross Profit / Net Sales
Revenue % = Net Sales / Overall Net Sales
